Shitpost (plural shitposts), noun
1. A worthless post on a messageboard, newsgroup, or other online discussion platform. Usually inane.


#Shitpost is an online community of like-minded friends and fellows, formed from the former online communities of numerous imageboards created by original founder and shitpost-original, Illu. Up until late 2015, early 2016, #shitpost boasted an active channel on Rizon and a continuously growing base of people, as well as several new services made to entertain and delight users. However, following a steep dropoff in activity, #shitpost slowly became inactive until late 2017, when it was revived by longtime member, Dredd // Rosemary. Since then, #shitpost has reformed with a particular dedication to its roots, and several services for its members are being built up.

Within the early months of 2018, we hope to have the following goals accomplished:

  • A functioning imap email service.
  • A set usenet group to congregate at.
  • A fully-staffed mod-team.
  • Twitter.
  • Much, much more.
